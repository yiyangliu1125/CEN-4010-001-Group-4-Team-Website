Video Game Deconstruction

I always enjoy a good video game every once in a while. However, I usually like to deconstruct them and view the original code that was used to create the game. Then, I tend to go off on a tangent and try to modify values to either mod the game or create a new one entirely. I will do this with any game I pick up such as Super Mario Bros. or Minecraft.

Harmonics

Harmonics is an interesting field that has to do with pitch shifts and combinations to create a sound. To create these sounds, I have to plan each note to hit at a specific time and duration to give the feeling I want to portray through the music. Technique and emotion within a piece shows two side of a coin. On one, discipline is needed to clearly state what one is playing. For the other, there is creativity in how to play and create what you are trying to go for.

Communication and Attention

Magic is an old hobby used to work on my communication and attention holding skills. Communication is needed to direct the other person's line of thought in the direction you want to lead. Attention holding is needed to keep the conversation on whatever you are trying to draw attention to. A combination of both allows me to both convey and lead people towards the goal I want to achieve.
