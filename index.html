About

The Freelancers are a group of programmers created from the forge of the Principles of Software Engineering class at FAU. This group was founded to complete the milestones ahead to give us a better understanding of how software engineering works and why it is needed.

Our group is composed of four people each skilled in some form of programming. We are able to accomplish most things by learning, comprehending, and performing each task to help further our knowledge.
